The Classics Club

Salvete, readers! We’re happy to introduce Pierrepont’s first ever Classics Journal! Since Pierrepont’s curriculum extensively investigates the languages, cultures, and societies of the ancient classical world, we decided to showcase our students’ incredible work relating to their Latin and Greek studies. Through translations, analyses of texts, short stories, and artworks, Pierrepont students have demonstrated passionate interest in the Classics, thoughtful inquiry, and inspiring creativity. We would like to acknowledge the following faculty members for their help and support: Bruce King, Téa Beer, Marya Fisher, Daniel Schwartzberg, and Julio Bernard. Without them, publishing this journal would not have been possible. We hope that you enjoy the Classics Journal of 2021-2022!

Sincerely,

Ian, Alexis, and Sarah
